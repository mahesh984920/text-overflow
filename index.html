Tendulkar is known as the "God of Cricket" or "Master Blaster". He is the highest run-scorer in both ODI and Test cricket, and the only player to score 100 international centuries. He also holds the record for the most player of the match awards in international cricket.

Virat Kohli

Hover for description...

Kohli has won all three ICC awards in a single year (2018), and is the only Indian to score 6 test hundreds in Australia after Sachin Tendulkar. He is also the highest run-scorer in the Indian Premier League (IPL) and the only player to have played all seasons for one team (Royal Challengers Bangalore).
